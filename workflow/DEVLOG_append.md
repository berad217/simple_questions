### Key Decisions Made

**Decision**: Frame the conflict as "High-Uncertainty vs Closure-Seeking"

- **Rationale**: Avoids "Man/Woman" or "Logical/Emotional" stereotypes. It focuses on the cognitive need (closure vs openness).
- **Impact**: Non-pathologizing for both sides.

**Decision**: Explicitly address "Do you love me?"

- **Rationale**: This is the "final boss" of high-uncertainty questions. Providing a cheat code ("I choose you") is high-leverage.
- **Impact**: Gives readers a concrete tool for their most stressful interactions.

**Decision**: Introduce "Layer 2" as the dominant layer in intimacy

- **Rationale**: Explicitly stating that "Accuracy < Connection" in this context gives permission to be less precise.
- **Impact**: Relieves the pressure to be "right" about feelings.
